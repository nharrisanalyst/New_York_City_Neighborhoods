New York City Neighborhood's Racial Demographics
NYC is one of the most racially diverse places in the world. In NYC One can go from neighboorhood to neighborhood and feel like they are in a whole new country. This interactive allows one to explore NYC's Racial Demographics. Click on a differnt neighborhood in the treemap(on the left/top(mobile)) or select different boroughs and neighborhoods on the bar chart (on the right/bottom(mobile)) to discover the different racial makeup and demographic trends in one of the worlds greatest cities.
Author: Nathan Harris
data source is Table PL-P3A NTA: Total Population by Mutually Exclusive Race and Hispanic Origin - New York City Neighborhood Tabulation Areas*, 2010
